About Us.

Summer Lawyers.

We are a law firm for lenders.

Established in 2013. From my understanding, it was started more like a general practice, did whatever work he could get. Then, slowly began to develop it into a mortgage specialist firm once he built up a reputation within the industry and became known by Lenders.

Growing from working out of a home office to having 40+ staff that services Lenders State-wide and become a name within the industry as a law firm that specialises in mortgage law. Whilst doing this, we have tried to stay true to who we are, which really reflect PR’s approach that made us successful being simple, easy going, relaxed, uncomplicated, efficient, timely, available and commercial.

I’m not really sure what to say here. Our firm has gone from strength to strength, I remember a good week in 2015 was if we settled 10 loans. Now, we settle more than that in 1 day (when times were good prior to Covid-19). We have become more experiences,, developed better relationships with Lenders / Brokers as well as with the Court via the use of highly reputable Barristers in our comprehensive litigation history representing Lenders on mortgage recovery matters. We started with a handful of Lenders, including, First Mortgage Capital Pty Ltd, which remain our main clients today. PR is well known. People who his never met, never acted for, have weirdly heard of him. Not sure what that means – but I think it is to a degree indicative of how present his made himself (in turn SL) in the industry.

    Our Litigation practice currently has an exciting opportunity for a Litigation Lawyer to join our team. This role is full time and based Sydney office. You will join a busy, high performing and collaborative team and will work with industry leading property developers, property investors and strata stakeholders.

    Duty

    In this role, you will act on property-related commercial disputes and litigation across a range of issues such as management contracts, leasing disputes, general property disputes and investigations. Specifically, your key matters will be focused on strata and community title disputes. You will be involved in running significant matters including drafting correspondence, preparing advice's, day to day management of files, briefing and liaising with counsel, preparing witness and expert evidence and assisting with the preparation of final and interlocutory proceedings.

    Skills

    To be successful in this role, you will have:

    • 2+ years PQE
    • Commercial and/or property litigation experience
    • Familiarity with Queensland Courts litigation
    • The ability to work independently and autonomously
    • Strong academic achievements
    • Highly developed interpersonal & communication skills
